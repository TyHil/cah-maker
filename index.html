Instructions

  1. Write and download all of the cards you want.
  2. Convert your downloaded SVG images to PNG images at 300 DPI using something like Inskscape.
  3. Go to MakePlayingCards.com, set the "Card stock" dropdown to "(M31) Linen", and proceed through the order process, selecting "Different images" where applicable.

Quick Answers

How do I remove a line?

Just leave it blank.

Why does the font look wrong?

Helvetica Neue Bold may not be installed on your device. You'll need to download and install it from somewhere like Fonts geek.

What should I use to convert from SVG to PNG?

I use Inskscape as it allows setting a high DPI like 300.

How do I order from somewhere besides MakePlayingCards.com?

These are cards are made according to the template provided by MakePlayingCards.com. If you'd like to use a different manufacturer, just make sure they accept the same specifications.
