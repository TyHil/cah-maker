Instructions

  1. Design and download all of the faces of the cards you will be ordering here. These are made according to the template provided by makeplayingcards.com here
  2. Convert your downloaded SVG images to PNG format at 300dpi.
  3. Go to makeplayingcards.com, set the "Card stock" dropdown to "(M31) Linen", and proceed through the order process, selecting "Different images" where applicable.

Quick Answers

How do I remove a line?

Just leave it blank.

The font looks off

Helvetica Neue Bold may not be installed on your device. You'll need to download and install it from somewhere like Fonts geek.

What should I use to convert to PNG?

I use Inskscape as it allows setting the DPI and much more.
