Instructions

  1. Design and download all of the faces of the cards you will be ordering here. These are made accrording to the template provided by makeplayingcards.com here
  2. Convert your downloaded SVG images to PNG format at 300dpi.
  3. Go to makeplayingcards.com, set the "Card stock" dropdown to "(M31) Linen", and proceed through the order process, selecting selecting "Different images" where applicable.
